About Me

The story behind the $300 bra and what it taught me about marketing.

From Intimates to Global Brands

Early in my career, I worked with a luxury lingerie brand where I had to convince customers that a $300 bra was worth the investment. This wasn't just about selling underwear—it was about selling confidence, craftsmanship, and self-worth.

That experience taught me the fundamental truth of marketing: people don't buy products; they buy better versions of themselves. Whether it's a luxury bra, a media subscription, or a high-end appliance, the psychology remains the same.

This insight became the foundation of my approach—combining data-driven strategy with human-centered storytelling to create marketing that doesn't just sell, but resonates.
