Community Impact Initiatives

Community Fridge Initiative

AJ Madison

Spearheaded corporate partnership program to support community refrigerators across New York City, addressing food insecurity at the neighborhood level while building meaningful brand connections.

Key Achievements:
  • Partnered with 12 community fridge organizations across 5 boroughs
  • Featured in Modern Luxury Magazine for community leadership
  • Developed sustainable partnership model that continues to operate today
  • Increased brand visibility while addressing critical community needs

Approach: Built bridges between corporate resources and grassroots organizations, creating mutual value while maintaining the authenticity of community-led initiatives.

Program Lead 2020-2021

Royal Chances Reentry Foundation

Nonprofit Marketing & Branding

Leading marketing and branding initiatives for a nonprofit dedicated to supporting individuals transitioning back into society after incarceration.

Services Provided:
  • Job readiness & mock interviews
  • Felony explanation letter guidance
  • Peer mentorship & life-skills training
  • Support navigating housing & resources

Mission: "You Don't Have to Be Ashamed — We're Here to Help. We believe in second chances, not second-guessing your worth. Your story isn't over; it's just being rewritten."

Unlocking Second Chances, Restoring Dignity.

Marketing & Branding Lead 2023-Present

Adult Literacy Tutoring

Imani House

Provided one-on-one and small group literacy tutoring for adults, helping them develop essential reading and writing skills for personal growth and professional advancement.

The Imani House Mission:

"To Assist Marginalized Youth, Families & Immigrants to Create Vibrant Neighborhoods Where Residents Are Decision Makers who Take Responsibility for the Improvement of their Lives and Surroundings."

We Believe that Everyone Can Succeed if They Are Motivated and Have Access to Appropriate Skills, Information and Opportunities.

Approach: Created personalized learning plans based on individual needs and goals, focusing on practical literacy skills that could be immediately applied to daily life and employment situations.

Adult Literacy Tutor 2018-2019

Food Insecurity Awareness Campaign

Community Outreach

Created comprehensive awareness campaign that educated corporate stakeholders about food insecurity while driving tangible support to local organizations.

Campaign Impact:
  • Developed educational content reaching 50,000+ corporate employees
  • Facilitated corporate donations to 8 local food justice organizations
  • Built volunteer programs engaging 200+ corporate volunteers
  • Established ongoing corporate sponsorship relationships

Strategy: Combined data-driven storytelling with actionable engagement opportunities, transforming awareness into meaningful community support.

Campaign Director 2020-2022
